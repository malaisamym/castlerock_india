SOLARO

Product Overview

SOLARO provides age-appropriate, curriculum-aligned lessons, activities, exercises, and quizzes for grades 3 to 12 in Math, Science, and English Language Arts. SOLARO is 100% curriculum-aligned, with courses designed to specifically address the current educational standards in all 50 US states, the new US Common Core State Standards, and three Canadian provinces (Alberta, British Columbia, and Ontario). We have combined study guides, interactive practice questions, and online tutoring into a comprehensive and fully integrated study solution that works across multiple platforms and is easily accessible wherever you are.

  • The Best Practice Quizzes
  • Automatic To-Do List
  • Create Your Own Flashcards and Notes
  • Easy-to-Learn Lessons
  • Simple Account Management
  • Parent Account
  • Reporting for Students, Parents, and Teachers
  • Teacher Account with In-Depth Reports
  • Automatic Assignments
  • Assign Homework Easily

Available for Web, iOS, Android

Motuto

Product Overview

Connect to a real-live tutor with motuto, Castle Rock Research's on-demand tutoring app. A service aimed at students in grades 6-12, motuto provides mobile homework assistance where you want it and when you want it. When you get stuck on a problem, or need help with a lesson, use your mobile device to connect to a live tutor who will explain the concept and walk you through the problem-solving process.

Motuto is currently available in all countries worldwide with access to the mobile app stores, but our app is not multilingual and all our sessions are conducted in English.

Featured in Tech Crunch

  • Available 1100h to 0300h EsT Mon to Fri
  • Available on iOS and Android
  • Available for mathematics and sciences
  • Sessions are stored on your device

Available for Web, iOS, Android

Classroom Response

Product Overview

Increase engagement in your classroom, lecture, or presentation with a clicker app that goes way beyond just multiple-choice questions for only a fraction of the cost of dedicated systems. Through true-or-false, numerical-response, multiple-choice, written-response, and even drawing right on the touch screen, your classes will now enable everyone to share and collaborate in a way never before possible.

  • Five question types
  • Ability to import images
  • All students can participate (Android, iOS)
  • Teacher can see who has logged in
  • Teacher can see who has answered
  • Fun and engaging

Available for iOS, Android

Integrity

Product Overview

INTEGRITY is an online application that performs detailed analyses of multiple-choice assessments. The comprehensive, easy-to-use reports generated by INTEGRITY offer unique professional development opportunities to clients striving to create quality assessments for their students. INTEGRITY also provides leading-edge collusion (cheating) detection features that allow clients to investigate the academic integrity of students taking multiple-choice tests.

Castle Rock Research - India (CRRI) is a Mobile technology solutions company providing comprehensive range of solutions & services for Mobile Phones and Web based products. Our team comprises of experienced designers, technologists & strategists driven by the passion to build meaningful technology solutions for web, mobile & multi-touch devices with primary focus on design, simplicity & performance.

Our consulting-driven approach makes it a strategic partner to several Fortune 500 enterprises like SAP, Vodafone, Samsung. We are sought out for providing ROI to our clients by reducing the time taken in product development & the development cost thereby ensuring higher profitability.
